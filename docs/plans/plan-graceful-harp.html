Why this plan exists

Pivot off "spec-only, no code" discipline. A Claude Design export landed at visuals/claude-design-exports/ that captures the app end to end.   • Playwright screenshot verification per route
  • Seeding docs/Solution Design/
Out of scope

  • Authentication, role gating, real RBAC
  • Real AI calls (banners are static)
  • Salesforce or Jira integrations
  • Edits to PRODUCT_SPEC.md or its subfiles
