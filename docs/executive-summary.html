SF Consulting AI Framework

Internal AI-powered delivery platform for Salesforce consulting engagements

The Problem

Every new Salesforce engagement starts with days of manual setup and weeks of inconsistent discovery. Context lives in meeting notes, Slack threads, email chains, and individual consultants' heads. When multiple BAs run discovery across client departments, nothing connects what they learn to the technical work it drives. Developers get a story title and some acceptance criteria, then spend hours hunting for business context. When we roll off, most of that hard-won project knowledge leaves with us.

How It Works

  • Progressive Knowledge Brain — AI ingests every transcript, discovery note, question, and decision, then synthesizes them into a continuously-refined knowledge base that gets smarter throughout the engagement.
  • Centralized Discovery — Multiple BAs work in parallel while the AI automatically organizes, links, and flags conflicts or gaps across everything they capture.
  • Developer Context Packages — When a developer picks up a ticket, they get the full story: business rationale, related decisions, org components, in-flight work on overlapping areas, and Salesforce guardrails — assembled automatically.
  • Automated Deliverables — Status reports, training materials, and knowledge transfer docs generated directly from accumulated project intelligence.

Value to the Firm

Lower Costs

Eliminates days of manual discovery setup per engagement. Replaces separate PM tooling. AI processing runs at ~$1–4 per transcript — negligible against the hours of manual extraction it replaces. Firm absorbs AI costs as overhead, not per-project expense.

Faster Delivery

Parallel discovery without context fragmentation. Developers get complete business context at ticket pickup instead of hunting for it. AI-assisted story generation with automatic consistency validation. Sprint conflict detection prevents developer collisions in real time.

Smarter Over Time

Three-layer knowledge architecture (structured data, AI-curated articles, semantic search) accumulates and refines project intelligence continuously. The AI reads its own previous synthesis and builds on it — compounding returns across the engagement.

Knowledge Transfer

Auto-generates handoff and training documentation from accumulated knowledge. When we roll off, the client retains comprehensive, machine-readable documentation of every decision, process, and component mapping. Archived projects reactivate with full context for Phase 2 engagements.

Designed to support 10–30 concurrent projects and 50–150 active consultants within the first 12 months.
